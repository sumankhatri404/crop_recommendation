About Us

The team "IT-Elite" is composed of two members. We have developed the crop recommendation system. The system will predict the recommnended crops as per the requirement of fertiliser and other soil properties as input parameters.

Crop yield prediction is an important aspect of agriculture that helps farmers make informed decisions about their crops. It involves estimating the number of crops that will be produced in a given area based on various factors such as soil type, weather conditions, and crop management practices.

Accuracy Level

Smart Crop Recommendation System consists of various ML classifiers such as RFC, KNN, NV, DT, that are used to make the predictions and all can recommend a crop accurately with the accuracy in the average range of 97% to 99%.

Client Satisfaction

Crop recommendation is a main tool for coping with different adversities in farming which is an innovation in rural areas. This study proposes an index for measuring farmer's satisfaction levels with crop prediction.
